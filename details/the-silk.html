Simpler, Simpler, Simpler

The Silk owner briefed us that most of her customers prefer to do businesses offline, visiting the store and conversing directly with her in-house designer. The primary use case for the website is to find out the business hour, direct contact for appointment, and how to get there. The website should be simple — the simpler, the better.

One of a Kind

One gown, one design, one customer... that’s how The Silk maintains uniqueness across its creations. They decided not to showcase its collection online to avoid illegal copy of its design (intellectual property violation is unfortunately not an uncommon issue in Indonesia). They would to show only one sample photo on the site with a periodic update.

Client

The Silk Wedding Boutique
Jakarta, Indonesia

Description

The Silk wanted to have a simple website as an extension of its presence as one of the best wedding gown boutiques in Jakarta.
